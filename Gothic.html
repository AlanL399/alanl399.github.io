Notes

  1. Sources are taken primarily from two places: the Gothic Breviary, and the Missa gothica, which contains the festal offices. The Mozarabic site offers the Breviary in web form, albeit with a few typos. Additional resources may be found on the site.
  2. Secondary sources on the notes of the office comes from the rubrics themselves, the Catholic Encylopedia, the 1593 and the 1603 notes.
  • Ante & Post: Raw from the Missa gothica.
  • Post Laudes: Added 'Dominus sit ...' to Alma Redemptoris. No 'Dominus sit ...' after Compline? Throughout the year, sources say default to Salve, but the accompanying prayer is unknown. Concede? But there is also the distinct option of using Roman: Omnipotens.
  • Psalms 50, 148, 149, 150, and the Benedictus are different between Breviary and the Missa gothica.
  • Currently, the pages use the Breviarium, except for psalms 3, 50, and 56, which are from the Psalter in the Breviarium. I.E., not the Missa gothica.
  • There is the issue of whether psalms end with Gloria, et honor, ... Sicut erat ..., or Gloria, et honor, ... in sæcula sæculorum. The Missa gothica contradicts the Breviarium on this point, as Sicut only appears at most eight times in the whole Breviarium. I am leaning towards not including it, as it seems like a Romanization. The Missa gothica itself is not consistent. For example, the Benedictus is given five times, and each time is slightly different. And it seems that, unlike Roman and in the Breviary, a Gloria is added after the Benedictus.
  • But as of yet, wherever Sicut is, it is kept.
  • Commemorations need to be clarified.
  • Oratio Conclusion: will default to 'Per misericordiam tuam ...'
  • DOM_PRIMA_ADVENTUS_LAUDIBUS. Capitula: 'Per Christum' fragment. It could be expanded to 'Per Christum Dominum nostrum', but this is unknown. Only instance in Breviary.
  • DOM_PRIMA_ADVENTUS_VESPEROS_II. Sono: Usually, it is from Vesperos I (or proper), but differs here. Added a link to Vesperos I, if it was a typo. Only instance in Breviary.
  • MON_PRIMA_ADVENTUS_MATUTINUM. Antiphon 1: Changed to match style.
  • MON_PRIMA_ADVENTUS_VESPEROS_II. Added Humiliate before blessing.
  • WED_PRIMA_ADVENTUS_MATUTINUM. Antiphon 2: Changed to match style. Same as above Monday. Antiphon 3: Differs than others in style.
  • FRI_PRIMA_ADVENTUS_MATUTINUM. Antiphon 2: Style is slightly different.
  • FRI_PRIMA_ADVENTUS_LAUDIBUS. Lauda: 148-149-150 is assumed.
  • MON_SECUNDA_ADVENTUS_LAUDIBUS. Lauda: 148-149-150 is assumed.
