Ad Medium Noctis

Choices and Changes:

  • There are 12 hours in total. 7 plus Aurora (Peculiaris), and 4 night hours.
  • In general, braces are self-inserts.
  • Beginning pulled from MS 2668 Gloss
  • Self-insert of Dominus sit, etc., is from the modern Tradition B.
  • All Psalms end with Gloria ... Sicut ... , unlike the modern rite, which leaves out the Sicut, etc.
  • The conclusion is also a self-insert from the modern Tradition B.
  • Oratio: Sunday option in gloss of MS 2668 & 30851.
  • Lauda: Last Alleluia in braces are a self-insert (to be consistent with Trad B).
  • Hymn: Doxology simply notes "Presta."
  • Verse: Fiat domine. (Ps 32.) Kirrieleison, kk.
  • Embolism: Self-insert, with options: 1st is Trad B, 2nd is from Mozarabic Site (Aurora).
  • We have a missa of canticles. It is not clear when there is a Gloria. It states: (last canticle) Gloria. 1st Canticle, Sicut, 2nd & 3rd Canticle. This is interpreted to: finish the canticle, Glora, 1st line of canticle, Sicut, 1st line of 2nd and 3rd canticles.
  • Prayer of Manasses: KJV. Cf. Byzantine Great Compline.
  • The following rubric is unclear: Deinde recitabis VIIII psalmos: et III (alios) canticos et sic dicis istos responsis.
  • Responsory style from Tradition B.
  • Lesson reponses from Tradition B.
  • The Clamorings are vague. It is unclear whether 2 are said sequentially or given as options. The 2nd seems have the repond, "Kyrie, miserere" from the bottom of MS 2668. Otherwise they end with "k." Otherwise the 2nd seems to correspond to a Miserationes (Mercy Prayers).
  • Dismissal from Tradition B.
