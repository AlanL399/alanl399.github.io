  1. This page catalogues the Ordinary Roman hebdomadal chants. Textual sources are the 1906 and 1888 Roman Breviaries.
  2. The chant texts are from the Tridentine Use, before the 1911 Divino Afflatu reforms. As such, modern chant musicology has only tangentially restored them, and consequently, many of the extant printed sources are of the 'corrupt' style.
  3. The Tones are subject to change but ultimately aims to be accurate.
  4. Compline is not listed as it already extant. The two antiphons, Miserere & Salva nos, are extensively documented and restored.
